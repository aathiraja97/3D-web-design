Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom tailoring for all outfits?

Yes, we specialize in custom tailoring for Kurtis and Chudi sets based on your unique measurements.

How do I provide my measurements?

You can provide your measurements through our detailed measurement guide, video call consultation, or by visiting our physical store.

What is the typical delivery timeline?

Standard deliveries take 7-10 business days. Custom orders typically take 14-21 days depending on complexity.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

What fabric types do you use?

We use premium fabrics including silk, cotton, linen, and blend materials. All fabrics are sourced for quality and durability.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for ready-made items. Custom orders are non-returnable unless there's a defect.

How can I contact your support?

You can reach our support team via email, WhatsApp, or phone. We typically respond within 24 hours.
